You have 6 "guess tokens".
Each guess uses up one token.

Each creature is a "round".
Guesses will be in turn.
First to guess each round simply rotates forward through the guessing order.

All guesses are public.
Two players cannot guess the same thing.

At the end of round each guess token that earned you any points is returned to you.
Any guess tokens that do not earn you any points will only return to you after next round (aka those tokens skip 1 round).

**Points**
__Size__ [can only guess __one__ per player per round]
- Exact hit: 4 points
- Adjacent hit: 1 point
> What counts as adjacent: if the correct size is ``medium``, adjacent would be ``small`` or ``large``.
> All existing sizes are in order:
> - Tiny (example: cat)
> - Small (example: dog, eagle)
> - Medium (example: wolf, human)
> - Large (example: horse)
> - Huge (example: elephant, killer whale)
> - Gargantuan (example: blue whale)

__Level__ [can only guess __one__ per player per round]
- Exact hit: 10 points
- Adjacent hit: 3 points (range of 3)
> What counts as adjacent: if the correct level is ``17``, then the "adjacent" levels would be ``14-16`` and ``18-20``.
> The level range is -1 to 25.

__Traits__ [can  guess __multiple__ per player per round]
- 5 point traits: Aberration, Amphibious, Animal, Aquatic, Beast, Celestial, Construct, Dragon, Dinosaur, Elemental, Fey, Fiend, Fungus, Incorporeal, Ooze, Plant, Skeleton, Swarm, Troll, Undead
- 7 point traits: Acid, Air, Angel, Cold, Daemon, Demon, Devil, Earth, Electricity, Fire, Genie, Ghost, Ghoul, Giant, Holy, Metal, Mindless, Monitor, Mummy, Nymph, Poison, Spirit, Sprite, Unholy, Water, Wood
- 9 point traits: Arcane, Archon, Astral, Darvakka, Divine, Dream, Ethereal, Golem, Gremlin, Mutant, Mythic, Occult, Phantom, Primal, Protean, Psychopomp, Qlippoth, Sahkil, Tane, Time

__Rarity traits__ [can only guess __one__ per player per round]
- Common (1 point)
> While in an area it could live you will run into this fairly commonly.
- Uncommon (4 points)
> A human living their lives in an area where it lives will encounter enough in their lifetime to not be surprised by it.
- Rare (8 points)
> A human living their lives in an area where it lives very possibly won't encounter it in their lifetime.
- Unique (10 points)
> Only 1 exists.

__Special Speeds__ [can  guess __multiple__ per player per round]
Burrow: 5 points
Climb: 3 points
Fly: 4 points
Swim: 4 points

__Plane of Origin__ [can only guess __one__ per player per round]
The Material Plane cannot be guessed.
- Exact: 8 points
- Within the Group: 2 points

Planes by Group:
- Inner Sphere: Plane of Air, Plane of Wood, Plane of Water, Plane of Metal, Plane of Earth, Plane of Fire
- Outer Sphere: Astral Plane, Maelstrom, Abaddon, Hell, Axis, Boneyard, Heaven, Nirvana, Elysium, The Outer Rifts
- Parallel Planes: Ethereal Plane, Creation's Forge, First World, The Netherworld, The Void
